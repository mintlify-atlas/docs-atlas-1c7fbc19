## What is Tiket?

Tiket is a single-page, zero-backend web game built with pure HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript. There are no accounts, no real money, and no installs — just open the page and start playing. Every press of the **Try Your Luck** button awards a random fake dollar amount, and landing $300 or more triggers a burst of confetti.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the game">
    Visit [mr-sunset.github.io/tiket](https://mr-sunset.github.io/tiket) on any device — desktop or mobile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Try Your Luck">
    Each tap generates a random prize between $10 and $1,000 and adds it to your running balance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch for confetti">
    Hit $300 or more on a single ticket and confetti bursts across the screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset anytime">
    Tap the reset icon in the top-right corner to clear your balance and start fresh.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  All money in Tiket is completely fake. No real currency, purchases, or accounts are involved.
</Note>
